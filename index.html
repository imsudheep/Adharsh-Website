About Us

Our academics is based on the philosopy of teaching that can be related to real life and meets the standards of twenty first century skills. The school follows the Equitable Syllabus to prepare the students for the board examinations. It also provides a holistic program of co-curricular activities like yoga, chess, triway, dance, karate, abacus and drawing (Optional).
